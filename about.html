General Background

Ithiel MCH is a renowned private healthcare organization dedicated to delivering exceptional healthcare services. Within our organization, we house a specialized center that focuses on providing high-quality healthcare exclusively to women and children (MCH). At Ithiel MCH, we firmly believe in the power of collaboration and partnerships. We are always open to forging alliances with like-minded organizations and stakeholders who share our vision of creating a healthier society. By working together, we can leverage our collective expertise and resources to make a lasting impact on healthcare accessibility and quality.

MCH's Vision:

At Ithiel PLC, our vision is to be a leading provider of quality healthcare services for women and children. We strive to create a healthier future by focusing on their unique healthcare needs and continuously improving our practices to meet the highest standards of care.


Commitment to Accessibility:

We firmly believe that healthcare should be accessible to all, regardless of socioeconomic status or background. With this in mind, we actively work on implementing initiatives to improve healthcare access for those who are less fortunate, ensuring that everyone has equal opportunities to receive the care they deserve.


Collaborative Approach:

Collaboration is at the heart of our mission. We actively seek partnerships with other organizations and stakeholders in the healthcare industry to leverage collective expertise and resources. By joining forces, we can effectively address healthcare challenges, enhance our services, and make a greater impact on the communities we serve.
One of partnerships programs was On March 13, 2020, in partnership with the Female Students Association of AAU College of Health Sciences (FSA), organized a Sexual and Reproductive Health training session. The session was conducted for 200 female students from Derartu Preparatory School. This collaborative effort aimed to educate and empower young girls concerning their reproductive health.
