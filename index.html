Obstetrics and Prenatal Care

We provide comprehensive obstetric care, including prenatal check-ups, fetal monitoring, ultrasound scans, and screenings for the health and well-being of both mother and fetus.

Pediatric Care

We specialize in pediatric care for infants, children, and adolescents, offering routine check-ups, vaccinations, illness diagnosis and treatment, chronic condition management, and growth monitoring.

Neonatal Intensive Care

Our specialized NICUs provide critical care for premature and critically ill newborns, equipped with advanced technology and staffed by a skilled multidisciplinary team.

Welcome to Ithiel Mothers and children Hospital

Upon your arrival at our hospital, our dedicated customer service team will greet you warmly with a pleasant smile. They will be readily available to guide you, offering directions and valuable advice as required. Additionally, you can conveniently obtain your hospital card from them and proceed with your visit.
